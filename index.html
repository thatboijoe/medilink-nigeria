About This Project

MediLink Nigeria

A research-driven web-based health service delivery system.

Design & Implementation of an Improved Web-Based Service Delivery System in the Health Sector

This project addresses a critical gap in Nigeria's healthcare infrastructure. Despite ICT's proven potential, many facilities still depend on paper-based records, manual scheduling, and fragmented data systems — leading to long wait times, duplicated records, and poor care continuity.

The study was conducted in Kaduna State, Nigeria, combining a Design & Development Research (DDR) approach with mixed methods across a stratified sample of 110 healthcare workers and patients.

  • 1Identify barriers to ICT adoption in Nigerian healthcare
  • 2Evaluate impact of manual processes on patient wait times
  • 3Design a web-based system for appointment scheduling and digital records
  • 4Assess how web platforms enhance care coordination
  • 5Propose recommendations for sustainable digital health transformation
